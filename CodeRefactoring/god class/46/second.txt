        ### Remarks:
        - The majority of methods and constants were retained within the `Project` class due to their dependencies on the core functionalities of the class.
        - Properties management functionalities were delegated to `ProjectProperties`.
        - Target management functionalities were delegated to `TargetManager`.
        - Logging and event handling functionalities were encapsulated within the `ProjectLogger`.
